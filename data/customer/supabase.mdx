For Supabase, Upstash Redis provides a globally available, edge-friendly data
layer that fits into their architecture. Combined with custom pricing,
Professional Support, and HIPAA compliance, Upstash Enterprise further enables
Supabase to scale demanding workloads while maintaining predictable costs and
low operational overhead.

#### About Supabase

Supabase is the open-source Postgres development platform that enables
developers to build scalable applications without managing infrastructure. It
provides production-ready building blocks such as Postgres databases,
authentication, Edge Functions, real-time APIs, and storage, and is trusted by
thousands of teams running applications at a global scale.

#### Tech Stack & How Upstash Fits In

Supabase operates a highly distributed platform with significant global traffic
and bandwidth-intensive workloads. As part of its infrastructure, Upstash is
used to support latency-sensitive data access patterns, including powering
Supabase's Edge Functions.

Because Edge Functions execute close to end users, Supabase needed a data layer
that could be accessed globally with low latency, without introducing
operational complexity. Upstash's serverless model and multi-region read support
fit naturally into this architecture, enabling Supabase to serve edge workloads
with consistent performance across regions.

Upstash complements Supabase's existing systems, allowing the team to
selectively use the right storage layer based on access patterns and latency
requirements.

#### Why Supabase Chose Upstash

When building their edge architecture, Supabase evaluated several caching and
data solutions including ElastiCache, Redis Cloud, and DynamoDB. Upstash emerged
as the preferred choice due to its unique combination of capabilities that
aligned well with Supabase's requirements for edge workloads.

Upstash stood out for several key reasons: <b>easy global replication, a REST
API, sub-100ms reads, and strong uptime reliability</b>. These features made it
the ideal solution for powering latency-sensitive Edge Functions without
introducing operational complexity.

#### Building with Upstash

With Upstash as their data layer for edge workloads from the start, Supabase was
able to build a streamlined architecture optimized for both performance and
cost. Latency-sensitive edge workloads could rely on Upstash for fast, globally
available access right out of the gate.

This approach simplified Supabase's edge architecture from day one. Instead of
needing to build custom replication or caching layers, the team could rely on
Upstash's multi-region capabilities to handle global access patterns out of the
box.

#### Enterprise Scale and Compliance

After moving to Upstash's Enterprise plan, Supabase also gained more predictable
cost modeling at high bandwidth levels. With transparent, usage-based pricing
and unlimited <b>bandwidth at a fixed per-GB rate</b>, Supabase was able to
scale edge and global workloads without unexpected cost spikes.

Upstash's Enterprise offering further enabled Supabase to meet stricter
compliance requirements. With <b>HIPAA</b> support — including execution of a
Business Associate Agreement (BAA), aligned internal controls, and ongoing
compliance support — Supabase can confidently support healthcare-related
workloads without building additional compliance infrastructure internally.
